Hackathon Competition organized by AturKreatif 2021 is an annual program implemented by the Information Security and Assurance Club (ISAC).

I participated in this competition with two other group members, Atikah Hamid and Aisyah Hanafi.

This competition aims to test the creativity of the participants where 3 themes have been set by organizers which are cyberbullying, the authenticity of Covid-19 info and information security during Covid-19 pandemic.

Participants have to create an innovation to solve an issue related to the theme.

Our group, The Brigade, has chosen the theme of cyberbullying and we have created Cybullybot which serves as a medium to report victims of cyberbullying to the police.

This is the flow and procedure of our Cybullybot:

  1. Victims can click on the options 'flag this account as cyberbully' at the specific comment that was hurting and bullying them
  2. Cybullybot bot will notify the victims that they have alert the bully to take down the post in 24 hours.
  3. If the bully delete their harmful comment immediately, the comment will disappear and report will not be furthered.
  4. Victims can click on the options 'flag this account as cyberbully' at the specific comment that was hurting and bullying them
  5. 5. After victims submit the form, the bot will send the report along with the screenshot as evidence to the police station.
